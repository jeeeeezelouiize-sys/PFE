Our Services

The reliable corrosion protection solutions for industrial infrastructures we provide:

Design & Installation

CEREP designs and implements cathodic protection systems for both new and existing structures, with the aim of preventing corrosion of pipes, tanks, and other metal structures.

Maintenance & Monitoring

CEREP provides continuous monitoring and maintenance services for cathodic protection systems to ensure that they function optimally and to detect any problems promptly.

Engineering & Consulting

CEREP offers specialized consulting for the analysis of corrosion risks and for the definition of the best technological solutions for the protection of infrastructures, thanks to a team of experts and cutting-edge technologies.

Training & Support

CEREP also offers training and technical support to companies that need to deepen their skills in cathodic protection and corrosion management.
